1 The Format

Golden Expedition is a single-phase cross-server brawl blending PvE and PvP. The store page sells it as a 5–6 day event, but only three days are actual combat — the rest is matchmaking and recovery. For those three days we share a map with one enemy server and can teleport freely to either side and fight for as long as we like.

Eliminations are the bulk of the scoreboard — zeroing citadels and killing units on the enemy server outweighs everything else. The PvE objectives (Guardians, Elephant) are reliable daily floors; the PvP is where the event is won or lost.

3 Golden Guardians · hourly PvE
🛡️ Golden Guardian Every hour · from 01:00 Day 1
  • Spawn every hour at random map locations, starting 01:00 on Day 1.
  • Daily caps: 20 guardians for rewards and 15M points — whichever you hit first.
  • You get a mail notification when each daily is complete (one for rewards, one for points).
  • Tougher guardians spawn deeper in enemy territory — better rewards and you can deal more damage to them, so points come faster.
Zone 1
Baseline guardians. Easy clears near safe ground.
Zone 2
Harder, richer. More damage per hit → finish dailies quicker.
Zone 3
Hardest & best. Deep in contested space — go with cover.
Efficiency: push toward Zone 2/3 guardians once you have support nearby — the higher per-hit damage clears the 15M / 20-kill dailies far faster than farming Zone 1.
4 The Elephant · 6-hour boss
🐘 Elephant Every 6 hours
  • Spawns at 06:00 · 12:00 · 18:00 (server time). Unlike the Behemoth, you can hit it solo or by rally.
  • Alternates servers each spawn — usually starts on ours.
  • Daily caps: 16M points from damage, plus 2 rewards for kills.
  • +10M bonus points go to the server that lands the killing blow — worth coordinating a finish.
  • Already teleported to the elephant's server? Use the teleporter to send troops remotely instead of relocating your citadel.

5 Your Daily Loop
Teleport across immediately. Get onto the enemy server at the start of the day — don't wait.
Clear tribes. 5 stamina each. Most players carry ~150 stamina — burn stamina potions here, don't hoard them.
Finish Guardian dailies. Hunt hourly spawns until both mails (20 kills / 15M points) arrive.
Farm with T1 marches. Send T1 farmers to gather — never your T8/T7. Farming marches are far more exposed than military ones.
Pivot for the Elephant. Before each 6-hour spawn: recall farmers → switch to military → take your 16M.
Hunt off the Elephant. Once your points are in, fight the enemy server contesting it. Eliminations win the event.
Bubble before sleep. Shield up when you log off — getting zeroed or caught in a bad trade hands them the points.
6 Marches: Do & Don't

✔ Do

Farm with T1 marches — cheap and expendable. Switch to your real military for the Elephant and PvP. Join a rally if you're worried about being caught in the open.

✘ Don't

Don't gather with T8/T7 units — farming marches get picked off, and those troops are too valuable to feed the enemy. Don't sit unbubbled overnight.

Caught in the open? Jump into the rally — there's usually no time for the enemy to counter-rally, so the rally is the safe seat. Solo open-field marches are the ones that get traded badly.
7 How We Win

The Plan

  • Pressure first. Teleport across on the bell and stay aggressive — open tiles and cross-border tiles let us reach their farmers and citadels.
  • Bank the PvE floors. Guardians (15M) and Elephant (16M) are guaranteed points everyone should hit daily.
  • Win the killing blows. Coordinate Elephant finishes for the +10M server bonus.
  • Out-eliminate them. Eliminations are the majority of the score — zero their citadels, farm their farmers, protect ours by bubbling and rallying.
