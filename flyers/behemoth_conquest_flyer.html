! The Big Picture

Two empires (servers) clash over their Behemoths. Across the event we earn Awaken Runestones to grow and buff our Behemoth, then unleash it on the enemy server while defending our own. Both sides attack and defend in cross-server battles. The empire with the higher total score wins — but both kingdoms earn Behemoth Crystals, so it pays to play even from behind.

Three ways to score: (1) cultivate — donate runestones to grow our Behemoth, (2) defend our Behemoth, (3) attack the enemy's. Rewards split across Personal, Alliance & Kingdom rankings. Spend Behemoth Crystals in the Behemoth Store before the event ends — during Stellar Dynasty you instead trade Crystals for Conquest Points in the Conquest Store (40-level track).

⚔Stage 1 — MatchingDay 1

What happens

  • Our server is paired against an enemy server — Behemoth vs Behemoth for the whole event.
  • No invasions yet. This is the day to scout the enemy Behemoth (toggle "Enemy Behemoth" on the lineage screen) and plan donations.

Do today

  • Start the daily grind immediately (see Stage 2) — runestones earned now go straight into cultivation.
  • Note the King's plan: the invasion time & location must be locked before the end of Day 3.
🪨Stage 2 — Beast TamingDays 2–4

Goal

  • Earn Awaken Runestones from daily activities and donate them to grow our Behemoth's siege, health, and the lineage tree before the invasion.
  • Donating also tips Bloodline Purity (a live tug-of-war bar vs the enemy) our way and earns 10 personal points per runestone.

📅 Daily schedule — Trial of Scion

Four fixed 30-min windows/day that alternate between the two servers (paired-server example shown). Be ready to rally at the start of our windows.

01:00–01:30 04:00–04:30 11:00–11:30 19:00–19:30

Do each day (Days 2–4)

  • Trial of Scion — rally the guarded "Scion" map target (Elite, ~160K Swordsmen). F2P-friendly: Lü Bu + low-tier (T4/T5) troops clears it cheaply. Mission tiers: 20K / 50K / 100K / 150K damage.
  • Behemoth Cultivation exploration — pet / groom / entertain the Behemoth on the map. Mission tiers: 3 / 10 / 15 / 20.
  • Rally vs Tribes — join every called rally; cheap repeatable runestones. Tiers: 1 / 4 / 8 / 12.
  • Donate runestones as you bank them — don't hoard; cultivation compounds.

💰 Runestone income (per day)

  • Trial of Scion damage: 1 runestone per 100K Behemoth points, up to 250/day.
  • Scion final blow: a separate +400/day on top — so the Trial alone can give up to ~650/day.
  • Plus Exploration and Rally vs Tribes both drop more. Do all of them daily.

💥Stage 3 — Attack-DefenseDays 5–6

What happens

  • Each Behemoth invades the other server at the locked time & location. Each invasion lasts 90 minutes.
  • Our Behemoth marches on their Imperial City; theirs marches on ours. The invading Behemoth knocks away player citadels and destroys alliance buildings in its lane.
  • Be online for the window — this is where the bulk of the score (and personal points) is won.

🗺 Battlefield Map

■ Our citadels (S & W portals) ■ Enemy citadels (N & E portals) ■ Taking Behemoth damage

The Behemoth sits in the centre and hammers the nearest citadels — so the cities closest to it bleed durability. We rally it from our side; the enemy counter-rallies to break our rallies and defend. Citadels can't sit inside the teleport zone.

⚔ Attacking the enemy Behemoth

  • Only rallies damage it — solo marches and war machines do nothing. Each rallied troop adds 1,000 dmg/sec; a full 40-troop rally = ~40,000/sec (unit stack size doesn't matter — bodies do).
  • Hold rallies long. After 5 minutes continuous, damage ramps +500 every 15s — don't let them disband.
  • Relocate to the enemy server to join the offense (also clears the 1× Invasion mission).

❤ The stun / Undying Force cycle

  • At 0 HP the Behemoth burns 1 Undying Force stack, disbands every rally on it, dazes briefly, then revives to full. It dies only when all stacks are depleted.
  • A scouted Behemoth had 4 stacks → roughly 5 full zeroes to kill it. Coordinate fresh waves to re-zero the instant it revives.
  • Defenders: zeroing theirs scatters their rallies and stops it scoring — protect ours in turn.

✚Stage 4 — RecoveryDay 7+

Do this before the window closes

  • CLICK THE CLAIM BUTTON — you get a base 35% of lost units back, but only if you claim. People forget and lose all of it.
  • Push toward the 65% cap two ways: Recovery Tickets (earned in-event or bought with Empire Coins, max 20) and allies spending recovery tokens on you (you can't self-use them — ask in alliance chat).
  • No manual recovery after this stage ends. Also spend any leftover Behemoth Crystals first.
