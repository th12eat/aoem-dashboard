! The Big Picture

Two empires (servers) clash over their Behemoths. Across the event we earn Awaken Runestones to grow and buff our Behemoth, then unleash it on the enemy server while defending our own. Both sides attack and defend in cross-server battles. The empire with the higher total score wins — but both kingdoms earn Behemoth Crystals, so it pays to play even from behind.

Three ways to score: (1) cultivate — donate runestones to grow our Behemoth, (2) defend our Behemoth, (3) attack the enemy's. Rewards split across Personal, Alliance & Kingdom rankings. Spend Behemoth Crystals in the Behemoth Store before the event ends — during Stellar Dynasty you instead trade Crystals for Conquest Points in the Conquest Store (40-level track).

💥Stage 3 — Attack-DefenseDays 5–6

What happens

  • Each Behemoth invades the other server at the locked time & location. Each invasion lasts 90 minutes.
  • The invading Behemoth knocks away player citadels and destroys alliance buildings. It has unlimited range and ignores shields — it can hit any citadel on the map, so bubbling won't protect your city.
  • Be online for the window — this is where the bulk of the score (and personal points) is won.

⚔ Attacking the enemy Behemoth

  • Only rallies damage it — solo marches and war machines do nothing. Each rally deals 1,000 base dmg/sec — that's per rally, not per march in it. So more separate rallies = more damage; fill every rally slot and keep as many distinct rallies on it as possible.
  • Hold rallies long. After 5 minutes continuous, damage ramps +500 every 15s — don't let them disband.
  • Relocate to the enemy server to join the offense (also clears the 1× Invasion mission).

❤ The stun / Undying Force cycle

  • At 0 HP the Behemoth burns 1 Undying Force stack, disbands every rally on it, dazes briefly, then revives to full. It dies only when all stacks are depleted.
  • The Behemoth has 3 Undying Force stacks → roughly 4 full zeroes to kill it. Coordinate fresh waves to re-zero the instant it revives.
  • Defenders: zeroing theirs scatters their rallies and stops it scoring — protect ours in turn.

✚Stage 4 — RecoveryDay 7+

Do this before the window closes

  • CLICK THE CLAIM BUTTON — you get a base 35% of lost units back, but only if you claim. People forget and lose all of it.
  • Push toward the 65% cap two ways: Recovery Tickets (earned in-event or bought with Empire Coins, max 20) and allies spending recovery tokens on you (you can't self-use them — ask in alliance chat).
  • No manual recovery after this stage ends. Also spend any leftover Behemoth Crystals first.
