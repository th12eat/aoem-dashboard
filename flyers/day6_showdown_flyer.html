5 Game Plan
Win Condition: The kingdom with the most Kingdom Points (KP) at event end wins. KP carries over from the Battle Stage — our starting gap vs the leaders is TBD this cycle.
⚠ HEAVY RISK OF GETTING ZEROED. Call out incoming threats and reinforce where possible. Otherwise — BUBBLE UP.

Key Rules

  • Refineries can ONLY be attacked via rally — no solo marches, no war machines
  • Essence-farming marches: Any tier (T1–T9) with 50K+ units qualifies for Primordial Essence. Use this for low-risk marches only. All combat marches should be max tier, full capacity, ready for war.
  • Only one troop per player per refinery earns Essence
  • If you need to stay bubbled, do NOT attack anyone. If you do, re-bubble immediately. HIGH RISK OF BEING ZEROED.

Refinery Control (position TBD)

  • eRa — Lead alliance — take the two refineries nearest our corner. If we get early access, position on them before contest start and rally the instant they go live.
  • Myt — Take the next-closest pair of refineries to our corner; rally immediately at contest start.
  • REU — Hold our home refinery and support wherever needed.
  • eRà — Support wherever needed — specifically siege defense.

Taxi Rallies

  • R4+ creates rally aimed at enemy citadel across the map → ferries players toward refineries
  • Officers kick/disband to dump players near refineries — reinforce immediately
  • Critical for getting REU/Myt/eRà into position fast after contest start

Endgame (21:00 UTC)

  • Do NOT abandon a refinery — 800M only pays the holder at 21:00
  • Even a last-second flip = 800M. Rally hard to recapture.
  • At 21:00 refineries enter protection (can't be contested), but the battle isn't over. Open-field gathering of Primordial Essence continues for an estimated 2-4 hours.
  • Open-field phase is optional. Do NOT feed KP via eliminations. If you can't compete in open field, use low-tier 50K+ unit marches to stay safe (bubble up & don't attack).
6 Know Your Role

Krakens

Be in the main rallies and garrisons. Fight open field where necessary. You are the heavy hitters.

Fighters

Same as Krakens but avoid opposing Krakens — giving up eliminations costs KP. Play smart: stay in rallies and garrisons if possible, otherwise fight what you can handle.

Rally/Taxi Leaders

Run taxi rallies from 19:00 to ferry players to refineries. Launch attack rallies on enemy refineries when called. Kick/disband on command.

Siege Defenders

Dedicated to clearing incoming siege. Ignore the main objective until all siege is exhausted, then default back to Fighter/Kraken role.

Fillers

Perma-bubbled players only here to sneak Primordial Essence from refinery courtyards. Do NOT garrison. Do NOT attack anyone or you lose your bubble.

7 I Can't Participate?
  • Yes you can! Bubble up, attack nobody, and send low-tier 50K+ unit marches into the refinery squares to earn Primordial Essence.
  • If you don't want the Essence rewards, you can stay back on Era 8 — but know you could safely participate with minimal losses.
  • The open-field phase after 21:00 is also low-risk if you stay bubbled and don't attack.
