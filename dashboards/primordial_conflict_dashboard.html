Event Structure

Primordial Conflict is a 4-server round-robin KvK. Three days of 1v1 battles (Days 2–4) where you face each opponent once, followed by a battlefield selection by the leading kingdom (Day 5), and a massive 4-way showdown (Day 6). Day 7 provides partial troop recovery.

Core Loop

During battle days, you earn Primordial Dew by gathering at Alchemy Pools and fighting over Order Workshops (which open twice daily). Dew converts to Primordial Points for scoring. On the showdown day, Essence Refineries appear around the chosen Imperial City — holding these generates Primordial Essence (premium currency) and massive Kingdom Points.

Key Difference from TME This is a 4-way event, not 2-way. Round-robin scoring determines who picks the Day 6 battlefield. Workshop fights happen in 1-hour windows twice daily across 3 days. Cross-border tiles also open on the map during the Battle Stage, so enemy players can hunt gatherers — stay alert while farming.

Order Workshops (Days 2–4)

3 workshops spawn per contest at 04:00–05:00 and 16:00–17:00 UTC daily (1 hour each). They spawn in Zone 2 regions (1 per region, random placement) and alternate between your map and the opponent's map.

  • During contests, you can teleport to enemy cross-border camps
  • No cap on how many players fit inside — swarm them
  • Capture is fast with numbers (~12–15min with 100+ players)
  • Once fully captured, they cannot be un-captured
  • Completed capture: 600M Kingdom Points per workshop
  • Occupying at contest end: additional 200M Kingdom Points per workshop
  • After capture, sit inside to soak additional points; PvP to deny enemy points
  • After the 1hr contest ends, all troops on enemy map are auto-returned
  • Participants get 30K Dew per round (does NOT generate points)

Essence Refineries (Day 6)

Eight refineries spawn around the Imperial City of the chosen showdown kingdom. This is the main event.

  • Occupying kingdom gets 1M Kingdom Points per second
  • Each refinery grants 800M Kingdom Points at contest end to last holder
  • Players garrisoning/near refineries earn Primordial Essences per second
  • Combat on IC plots also generates Essence (capped)
  • 8 refineries × 800M = 6.4B points available at end alone

Days 2–4 — Battle Stage (Round Robin)

Each day, your kingdom is paired 1v1 against a different opponent. Matchups rotate daily with priority given to kingdoms that haven't faced each other yet. Two activities run simultaneously:

Alchemy Pool Gathering

  • Pools of levels 1–4 appear on the world map throughout the stage
  • Level 4 pools spawn near the Imperial City only, refresh every 12h, last 12h
  • Lv.4 pools are guarded by Corrupted Hyenas — must kill them first
  • Killing blow on a hyena grants +10% gather speed at that pool (tagging alone doesn't count)
  • Hyenas don't respawn — 1 per Lv.4 pool, refreshes every 12h with the pool. Only 3 Lv.4 pools found near IC
  • Lv.1–3 pools only allow 1 gatherer; Lv.4 pools allow up to 40 simultaneous gatherers
  • Minimum 50,000 units required per march to gather

  • You pick WHICH kingdom's map to fight on — huge strategic advantage

Currency System

The naming is confusing, so here's the clean breakdown. There are three currencies and one scoring system.

Primordial Dew
The primary currency with two tracked pools:

Personal Dew — what you individually gather. Converts to Primordial Points at a rate of 100 Dew → 10K Points. Also spent at the Alchemist Black Market and contributes to Alchemy Technology thresholds.

Kingdom Dew — server-wide aggregate total. Likely fills automatically as the whole server gathers. Used as a prerequisite threshold for Alchemy Technology nodes.
Sources: Alchemy Pools · Order Workshop occupation · Workshop participation (30K, no points)
Primordial Essence
Premium currency available only during the Day 6 Showdown. Refined from Dew at Essence Refineries. Earned by garrisoning or stationing near refineries, and through combat on IC plots (capped). Exchangeable at the Alchemist Black Market for higher-tier rewards.
Sources: Essence Refineries (Day 6 only) · Combat on IC plots (capped)
Primordial Points / Kingdom Points
The scoring system — not a spendable currency. Determines round-robin matchup results and who picks the Day 5 battlefield. Earned from Dew gathering (100 Dew = 10K pts), combat kills, workshop occupation, and refinery control. Kingdom Points are the server-wide total.
Score only · Not spendable · Determines battlefield selection

Points from Gathering

For every 100 Personal Primordial Dew gathered, you earn 10,000 Personal Primordial Points. The 30K Dew awarded for workshop participation does not generate points.

Points from Stationing

For every second of valid stationing in an occupied Order Workshop, earn 5 Primordial Dew (equivalent to 500 Points). Stationing is valid only when troop count exceeds the minimum requirement. Each player can have at most 1 troop validly stationed at a time.

Points from Combat

Combat is valid only when the tier difference between units doesn't exceed 2 tiers. Points scale by enemy unit tier:

Tier Gap Rule Combat only scores points if the tier difference is ≤ 2. So T9 vs T7 counts, but T9 vs T6 does not. This prevents point farming with throwaway low-tier troops.
