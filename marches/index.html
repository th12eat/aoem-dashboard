Reading a build: The lead hero of each march is the Commander — only they use their Commander Skill; the other two contribute their Signature & Secondary skills. Gear stars show ascension (★★★★ = 3 of 4; 4 = fully ascended), all gear at Lv.80. Secondary-skill red diamonds (◆◆◆◆) are scroll upgrades. Rarity colors: grey green blue purple gold red.
Gems — 3 per gear piece, shown with effect + value and a rarity dot (red = highest tier). Helm/Gauntlets/Chest run a matched set of 3; Boots mix one of each. A few sockets read from the ascend screen are marked TBD where the exact value wasn't legible.
