Hero skills · mechanic definitions · NPC & building glossary
How to read a skill: the effect is written in plain words; the green value in parentheses is the magnitude and how it scales. 480% scaling with Might = damage equal to 480% of the hero's Might attribute. A value marked (flat) does not scale. Heroes have four attributes — Might, Strategy, Armor, Siege.
Skills shown at max: Level 40 + full red diamonds (◆◆◆◆, raised with Legendary Skill Scrolls). Only Secondary skills have diamonds; Commander & Signature skills do not. Tap a dotted term for its definition.
